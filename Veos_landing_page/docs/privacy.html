Privacy Policy

How VERO AI collects, uses and protects data.

Data Collection

We collect only the information required to provide the service: files submitted for verification, account email, and minimal usage metadata.

Retention & Security

Files are processed transiently and retained only as required by your account settings. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. For production deployments, integrate with your own secure storage and key management.

User Controls

Users may request deletion of data or export through the account portal or by contacting support.
